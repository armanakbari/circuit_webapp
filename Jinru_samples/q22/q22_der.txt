The power dissipated in the $60 \mathrm{k} \Omega$ resistor is

$$
p_{601 \mathrm{k} \Omega}(t)=i_0^2(t)\left(60 \times 10^3\right)=60 e^{-50 t} \mathrm{~mW}, \quad t \geq 0^{+} .
$$


The total energy dissipated is

$$
w_{601 \mathrm{k} \Omega}=\int_0^{\infty} i_\rho^2(t)\left(60 \times 10^3\right) d t=1.2 \mathrm{~mJ}
$$
