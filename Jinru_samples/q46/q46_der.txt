To find $i_1$ we first note that

$$
3 \frac{d i_1}{d t}+6 \frac{d i_2}{d t}=6 \frac{d i_1}{d t}+15 \frac{d i_2}{d t}
$$

or

$$
\frac{d i_1}{d t}=-3 \frac{d i_2}{d t}
$$

It also follows $i_o=i_1+i_2$,

$$
\frac{d i_o}{d t}=\frac{d i_1}{d t}+\frac{d i_2}{d t}
$$


Therefore

$$
80 e^{-5 t}=-2 \frac{d i_2}{d t}
$$

Using Kirchhoff's current law we get

$$
i_1=24-24 e^{-5 t} \mathrm{~A}, \quad t \geq 0
$$
