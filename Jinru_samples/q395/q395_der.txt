For the input loop, KVL gives

$$
-4+I_B\left(20 \times 10^3\right)+V_{B E}=0
$$


Since $V_{B E}=0.7 \mathrm{~V}$ in the active mode,

$$
I_B=\frac{4-0.7}{20 \times 10^3}=165 \mu \mathrm{~A}
$$


But

$$
I_C=\beta I_B=50 \times 165 \mu \mathrm{~A}=8.25 \mathrm{~mA}
$$


For the output loop, KVL gives

$$
-v_o-100 I_C+6=0
$$

or

$$
v_o=6-100 I_C=6-0.825=5.175 \mathrm{~V}
$$
