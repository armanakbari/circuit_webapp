The resonant frequency is,

$$
f_0=\frac{1}{2 \pi \sqrt{600 \times 10^{-3} \times 80 \times 10^{-12}}}=22.97 \mathrm{kHz}
$$


The quality factor is calculated as,

$$
Q_s=\frac{1}{R} \sqrt{\frac{L}{C}}=\frac{1}{540} \sqrt{\frac{600 \times 10^{-3}}{80 \times 10^{-12}}}=160.38
$$


The bandwidth is calculated as,

$$
B W=\frac{f_0}{Q_s}=\frac{22.97}{160.38}=167.8 \mathrm{kHz} / \mathrm{s}
$$
