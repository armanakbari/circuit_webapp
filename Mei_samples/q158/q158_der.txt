The voltage $V_1$  can be determined as,

$$
V_1=(3+6) 1=9 \mathrm{~V}
$$

$$
I_0=\frac{12}{4}=3 \mathrm{~A}
$$


The current $I_1$ is calculated as,

$$
I_1=\frac{V_1}{3}=\frac{9}{3}=3 \mathrm{~A}
$$
