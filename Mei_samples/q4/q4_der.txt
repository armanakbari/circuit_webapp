\begin{aligned}
&\text { Applying KVL to the circuit in Fig. } 2.9 \text { yields, }\\
&-40-10+4+6+V_2=0\\
&V_2=40 \mathrm{~V}
\end{aligned}
The value of the current in the circuit is determined as,

$$
\begin{gathered}
V_2=I_s \times 4=40 \\
I_s=10 \mathrm{~A}
\end{gathered}
$$


The value of the resistance $R_3$ is determined as,

$$
R_3=\frac{V_3}{I_s}=\frac{6}{10}=0.6 \Omega
$$
