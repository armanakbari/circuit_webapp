$$
\begin{aligned}
\eta & =\frac{P_o}{P_s} \quad(\eta \text { in decimal form }) \\
\eta & =\frac{R_L}{R_{T h}+R_L} \\
\eta\left(R_{T h}+R_L\right) & =R_L \\
\eta R_{T h}+\eta R_L & =R_L \\
R_L(1-\eta) & =\eta R_{T h}
\end{aligned}
$$

and

$$
\begin{gathered}
R_L=\frac{\eta R_{T h}}{1-\eta} \\
\end{gathered}
$$


For the battery,

$$
R_L=\frac{0.75(0.5 \Omega)}{1-0.75}=\mathbf{1 . 5} \Omega
$$
