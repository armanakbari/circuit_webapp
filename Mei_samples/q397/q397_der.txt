By Eq. (6.10), the current divider rule,

$$
I_1=\frac{R_2 I}{R_1+R_2}=\frac{(4 \Omega)(12 \mathrm{~A})}{2 \Omega+4 \Omega}=\mathbf{8} \mathbf{A}
$$


Applying Kirchhoff's current law, and

$$
\begin{gathered}
I=I_1+I_2 \\
I_2=I-I_1=12 \mathrm{~A}-8 \mathrm{~A}=\mathbf{4} \mathbf{A}
\end{gathered}
$$
