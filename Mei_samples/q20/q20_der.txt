\begin{aligned}
&\text { The total resistance of the circuit is, }\\
&R_t=2+\frac{3 \times(1+5)}{3+6}=4 \Omega
\end{aligned}
The value of the source current is,

$$
I_s=\frac{60}{4}=15 \mathrm{~A}
$$


The branch currents $I_1$ and $I_2$ can be calculated as,

$$
\begin{gathered}
I_1=\frac{(5+1)}{6+3} \times 15=10 \mathrm{~A} \\
I_2=\frac{3}{6+3} \times 15=5 \mathrm{~A}
\end{gathered}
$$
