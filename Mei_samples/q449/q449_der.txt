Although it might appear that the network cannot be solved using methods introduced thus far, one source conversion as shown in Fig. 8.17 will result in a simple series circuit:

$$
E_s=I_1 R_1=(4 \mathrm{~A})(3 \Omega)=12 \mathrm{~V}
$$

and

$$
R_s=R_1=3 \Omega
$$

and

$$
I_2=\frac{E_s+E_2}{R_s+R_2}=\frac{12 \mathrm{~V}+5 \mathrm{~V}}{3 \Omega+2 \Omega}=\frac{17 \mathrm{~V}}{5 \Omega}=3.4 \mathrm{~A}
$$
