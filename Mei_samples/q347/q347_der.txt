Applying Kirchhoff's voltage law to the network  in the clockwise direction will result in

$$
60 \mathrm{~V}-40 \mathrm{~V}-V_x+30 \mathrm{~V}=0
$$

and

$$
\begin{aligned}
V_x & =60 \mathrm{~V}+30 \mathrm{~V}-40 \mathrm{~V}=90 \mathrm{~V}-40 \mathrm{~V} \\
& =\mathbf{5 0} \mathbf{V}
\end{aligned}
$$
