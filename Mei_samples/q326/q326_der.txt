The resonant frequency is calculated as,

$$
f_0=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \sqrt{\frac{1}{0.8 \times 0.9 \times 10^{-6}}-\frac{5^2}{0.8^2}}=187.56 \mathrm{~Hz}
$$


The quality factor is determined as,

$$
Q_p=\frac{2 \pi \times 187.56 \times 0.8}{5}=188.56
$$


Resonance impedance is calculated as,

$$
Z_r=\frac{0.8}{5 \times 0.9 \times 10^{-6}}=177.78 \mathrm{k} \Omega
$$
