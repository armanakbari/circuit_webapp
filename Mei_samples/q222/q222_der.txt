Under DC condition, the inductor is short circuited and the capacitor is open circuited. In this case, the circuit is shown in Fig. 5.31. The equivalent circuit resistance is,

$$
R_{\mathrm{eq}}=4+\frac{3 \times(5+1)}{3+6}=6 \Omega
$$


The source current is,

$$
i=\frac{30}{6}=5 \mathrm{~A}
$$
