For differential-mode operation, 

$$
\begin{aligned}
& V_X=-g_m\left(R_D \| r_{O 1}\right) \frac{V_{i n 1}-V_{i n 2}}{2} \\
& V_Y=-g_m\left(R_D \| r_{O 2}\right) \frac{V_{i n 2}-V_{i n 1}}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text { That is, }\\
&V_X-V_Y=-g_m\left(R_D \| r_O\right)\left(V_{i n 1}-V_{i n 2}\right)
\end{aligned}
